Introduction

Since her debut with her widely-acclaimed single "Ocean Eyes" in 2016, Billie Eilish has demonstrated great versatility and range within a soundscape that is still unmistakeably her own. Throughout her career, Billie has released three albums: WHEN WE ALL FALL ASLEEP, WHERE DO WE GO?, Happier Than Ever, and HIT ME HARD AND SOFT.
